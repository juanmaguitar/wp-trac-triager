# Privacy Policy for WP Trac Triager

**Effective Date:** February 9, 2026

## Data Collection

**WP Trac Triager does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

### What We Store Locally:
- Sidebar layout preferences (using Chrome's storage.sync)
- WordPress release version selection

This data stays in your browser and never leaves your device.

## Permissions
- `storage`: Saves your preferences locally
- `https://core.trac.wordpress.org/*`: Reads Trac pages to enhance display
- `https://meta.trac.wordpress.org/*`: Reads Meta Trac pages to enhance display

## Data Processing
All processing happens locally. No external servers. No tracking. No analytics.
